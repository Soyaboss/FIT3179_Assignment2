Public Transport Insights in Kuala Lumpur

This visualization offers a comprehensive view of three of Kuala Lumpur’s rail network: fare relationships between stations, a station map for spatial context, and daily ridership trends with interactive filtering. Explore the data to better understand public transport trends in Kuala Lumpur.

How do fares vary between origin–destination station pairs?

Explore the fare matrix across the various stations. Use the slider below the map to filter by price or to find routes with higher or lower fares.

Hover to see the exact Origin → Destination pair and fare value.

Where are the LRT stations located?

The map shows station locations and with line filters. This helps better understand the spatial coverage and proximity between LRT lines in Kuala Lumpur.

How has daily ridership changed over time?

Interact with the date slider/brush or mode filter to zoom into specific periods and compare LRT vs MRT lines. According to the graph we can see a massive decrease in 2020 due to the covid pandemic.
